How It Works

  1. Navigate to a song on SongSelect
  2. Click the bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar
  3. The bookmarklet fetches the ChordPro file from SongSelect
  4. Opens Setalight (or reuses existing tab) and imports the song
  5. Song is saved to your IndexedDB library
